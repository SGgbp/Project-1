Frequently asked questions

Do I need a website or a laptop to use SnapReply?

No. Everything runs through WhatsApp. You set up in 5 minutes by texting your SnapReply number. No dashboard, no browser, no laptop ever required.

What does the AI actually say to my customers?

It introduces itself as your assistant, finds out what service they need, collects their preferred date and time, and confirms a booking request — all in a warm, natural tone. You can customise the greeting. It never quotes prices or makes promises it can't keep.

What happens when the AI books something — does it go straight in my diary?

You get a WhatsApp notification instantly with all the details. On Growth and Pro plans, it also creates a Google Calendar event automatically. You confirm with the customer directly.

Can I pause it if I'm on holiday?

Yes. Just text PAUSE to your SnapReply number. Text UNPAUSE when you're back. Takes 2 seconds.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

You get full access to your chosen plan for 7 days with no charge. Your card isn't billed until the trial ends. Cancel anytime by texting STOP — no questions asked.

Is my customer's data safe?

Yes. We're GDPR compliant, ICO registered, and conversations are automatically deleted after 90 days. We never sell your data or your customers' data. Full details in our privacy policy.

What if the AI says something wrong?

SnapReply has built-in safety guardrails. If a reply looks like it could cause a problem (e.g. making a price promise), it holds the message and alerts you instead. You get a holding message sent to the customer automatically.

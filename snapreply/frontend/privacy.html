Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

SnapReply ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and that of your customers. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. Who we are

SnapReply is a trading name registered in England and Wales. We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). Our email for data enquiries is privacy@snapreply.co.uk.

2. Data we collect about you (business owners)

  • Name, email address, and phone number (provided at signup)
  • Business name, type, and location
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Usage data: login timestamps, WhatsApp commands sent, dashboard activity

3. Data we process about your customers

When your customers message your SnapReply number, we process:

  • Phone number (WhatsApp or SMS)
  • Name (if provided during conversation)
  • Conversation messages and booking details

We process this data on your behalf as a data processor. You are the data controller for your customers' data. You are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful basis for using SnapReply to communicate with your customers.

4. Data retention

Conversation histories and enquiry records are automatically deleted after 90 days. Booking records are retained for 6 years for accounting purposes, as required by HMRC. Account data is retained while your account is active and for 30 days after cancellation.

5. How we use your data

  • To provide the SnapReply service (AI replies, booking notifications, daily digests)
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To send service-related communications (trial reminders, payment receipts)
  • To improve the service (anonymised, aggregated analytics only)

We never sell your data or your customers' data to third parties.

6. Third-party processors

  • Supabase — database hosting (UK/EU data residency)
  • Meta (WhatsApp) — message delivery via WhatsApp Cloud API
  • Twilio — SMS and voice processing
  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Anthropic — AI inference (messages processed to generate replies)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Railway — application hosting

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@snapreply.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses no tracking cookies. We use only a session cookie (JWT token stored in localStorage) for authentication. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.

9. Security

We use TLS encryption in transit, bcrypt password hashing, JWT authentication, and webhook signature validation. We follow OWASP security best practices.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:

privacy@snapreply.co.uk
